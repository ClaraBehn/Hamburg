Harburg

A stroll through Harburg reveals beautiful half-timbered houses, noble art nouveau b uildings and historic industrial plants. High-performance scientists are conducting research at the Technical University of Hamburg and a new lively district is being b uilt in the inland harbour. At the same time, an above-average number of people with low incomes live here.

The people of Harburg are proud of their many historic buildings. During a walk through the city centre, one should definitely take a look at the magnificent town hall, which is decorated with foliage and tendrils made of sandstone. After its destruction in 1944, the people of Harburg rebuilt it in neo-Renaissance style. From there you can quickly get to Harburger Schloßstraße, where there are seven listed houses.
